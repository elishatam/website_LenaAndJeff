Strip Kim Jong-il's Power

Help Refugee Artist Expose Ex-Boss to the World

Song Byeok's 2011 gallery show, "Forever Freedom," received international press and acclaim. But without an online portfolio, he was unable to connect with new fans and potential buyers inspired by his life story.

Song is a former North Korean propagandist who now paints acrylic pieces satirizing Kim Jong-il. Before escaping, the artist endured torture at the hands of the very regime he once praised in his art. Song's crime: attempting to cross into China to find food. You can learn more about him here and here.
